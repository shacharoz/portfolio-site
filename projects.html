intel realsense extension for scratch

x close

This project offers Scratchers to play with Intel RealSense Technology using blocks, like: finger tracking, face expressions and speech recognition in their Scratch projects.

Read more at the product page or try for yourself in ScratchX

Tested on a few hundreds of kids so far. Expert Scratch users make use of the blocks right away. New Scratchers take a few minutes to understand Scratch concept, and manage to use the blocks in 10 minutes.

This project is supported by MIT Scratch team, Intel Education and Intel Realsense Team.

the last battle

x close

Aliens from Zork and Mortar planets try to take over Earth. The key battle occurs at Weizmann institute in Israel, since whoever controls that knowledge base can win the race. The teenagers play the alien groups, battling each other for the control of the science posts at the outdoor museum of Weizmann institute.

This location based game activity has one goal: to interest teenagers about science. This framework can be extended to other outdoor museums.


gameplay

Inspired by Google Ingress, Vanished and Environmental Detectives, here are the rules:

  • The kids receive a short brief from their commander. First they need to touch-base all posts they want to start with.
  • They receive a homebase. The winner is the side that controls more posts in one hour.
  • Take over a post by scoring higher than the other group. Get score by answering questions on that subject. The questions force the user to try the facility in a unique way.
  • From time to time, the instructor declares a special battle between the two groups on one post. These challenges are easier if you have more players with you.

    • Example activity: "Balance the highest number of kids on this swing in one minute"

  • Near the end, a third enemy (NPC) joins the battle and balances the game a bit. It takes over posts faster than the groups and eventually they will lose unless they join forces against the new enemy. Interested to know who is this powerful enemy? Call me :)

roseta's flowers

x close

Roseta’s flowers deal with the interpersonal relationships of a distant group of friends. Have you ever wondered how your friends are doing right now? Do they think of you as well?

Roseta’s flowers visualize the strength of the bond and silently encourage group members to act. In some ways, it is a physical private social network.


Solution

Taking care of your flower influences the other flowers. The flower pot changes its behavior (movement and sound) according to the amount of treatment it gets from the group. The more people treat it, the happier it becomes.

If one forgets about the flower for some time, the others will notice the change in behavior of their Roseta flower, and hopefully would try to contact each other to find out what happened. Therefore, the group is encouraged to reconnect just when the members need assistance.

    AUGMENTED REALITY INSTRUCTION EDITOR

    x close

    How often you face a home appliance, like washing machine or an office printer that you can't remember how to operate? ARIE solves us the dependency from opening those long instruction manuals, call a technician or even ask Google...

    ARIE gives you the step-by-step instructions in real time, focusing only on the task you wish to perform right now.


    Applications

    • Home environment: toy and furniture assembly; activating and fixing appliances.
    • Industrial factories: offers on the job training (OJT) right from day one. Saves a lot of money for the factory, both on the training course cost and for shortening the time the worker learns his role.

    SOLUTION

    Real-time instructions manual that presents the information right when and where it is needed. Project ARIE has two components: the viewer and the editor. The viewer offers the possibility to watch real-time instructions. It is the freeware side of the product. The editor is the tool which allows the creation of all that content.

    ARIE offers having a super-fast instruction, more personal, and in real-time, while the worker is active and actually working. ARIE saves time and money for the factory and the workers.

    living in the holy land

    x close

    King David

    Moses the prophet

    warrior

    “Living in the Holy Land” is both an educational game and a powerful teaching tool. It is a single player role-play adventure game that follows the biblical stories.

    The player experiences the story in person instead of just reading it. That leads to easier understanding of the surrounding, the environment and the conflicts of the characters, which allows the student to better identify with them.

    moses and the burning bush


    Educational Value and Context

    The game tracks and follows the player moves, and presents that performance in real time to the teacher and parent. It can be used both as a pre-learning tool to teach the story itself before discussing it in the classroom, allowing a significantly more rich debate; or as an in-depth research tool that allows further investigation of a specific issue or question. The game stages are parallel to bible chapters, allowing easy adaptation into the school’s curriculum.

    gameplay

    The game uses the role-play adventure mechanics (RPG) where player faces missions, quests and challenges where each accomplishment rewards the player with better equipment or avatar improvements.

    The game has several unique GUI elements:
    The smart sheep aids the player any time they are in need; the book is the source of all knowledge (contains relevant information on the current story from other aspects, like: philosophy, science, history and geography); the map assists in navigation; and the closeness-meter that shows how close the player’s current progression is to the original biblical story.

    research

    Thorough academic research has been conducted for teaching methods in Israel's public schools systems and what difficulties the students experience while learning the bible. Research showed that the goals set by the Israeli ministry of education are not met, mostly because the lack of time for the teacher in classroom.

    Some of these educational goals are: understand biblical language; understand biblical story and sequence of events; connect biblical places with nowadays places; understand environment challenges; understand the issues that faced the biblical characters;

    We believe that using a video game as a teaching tool can aid in reaching these educational goals.

    hoplites

    x close

    Help the Hoplites soldiers get back to the Olympus in this hand tracking casual game. Using hand postures and movements the player can bridge gaps and chasms for the soldiers or lift them directly to a destination.

    Hoplites is a 5 levels demo version, developed by the Intel Perceptual Computing Studio using the Intel RealSense SDK.

    My role was to turn a general game concept into a playable version with a few levels, inventing and creating the game elements and to come up with the instructional theory by which the player would learn the controls and the game rules.
