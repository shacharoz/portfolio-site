All Starts Within

Youth Empowerment Workshop

background

Adolescence brings some beauty, excitement and magic. But it comes with lots of challenges, doubts, pressure, expectations, achievements, decisions to be made and numerous changes.

we will set on a journey to discover ourselves, our aspirations and desires, develop awareness, insights, personal point of view and a way of thinking.

description

the workshop provides participants with practical tools to be used at different life stages and future decision making processes you will face in the future. the main goal is to get you more prepared and equiped for growth, acceptance, social skills, happiness, fulfillment, and self empowerment.

subjects

who am i

matters of the heart

its not simple to be simple

experience or not to be

thought create reality

the first part will converse about better understanding, paying attention and awareness to myself, who am i, my needs, what i am scared from, my strengths and weaknesses. we will talk about society and belonging, my limits and borders, and also on directing, understanding and controlling my emotions, changes in the mood. we will speak about meaningful experiences, and what their role in our life, what they do to us, what can i learn from it about life and myself.

creative thinking

moment to myself

great day

personal fulfillment

ending is also a beginning

the second part will deal more with fulfillment, accomplishing ideas, creative thinking, growth mindset, changing behavior patterns and paradigms. we will learn to face the environment and the challenges it confronts us with, as well as with those we put to ourselves. we will learn how to create a safe environment to myself, a social circle that is furtile and allowing me to grow continuously. we will define goals and doable steps, to keep a daily routine, nutrition and a healthy life style.
